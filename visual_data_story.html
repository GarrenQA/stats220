Evaluating Youtube Ads encountered by users

This analysis explores YouTube advertisements, focusing on features such as whether the ad is skippable, its length, and the type of content being promoted. The effectiveness of the ads on individuals (myself) can be observed by comparing how relevant the advertisements are using the data collected. It can help give insights into which format and content is relevant to me, whether it was engaging or intrusive.
